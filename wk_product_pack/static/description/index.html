PRODUCT PACK

Use Odoo product pack for cross-selling and upselling. bundle multiple items together to make a Odoo product pack such as beauty products pack, birthday gif pack, gym essentials pack, etc. The entire product pack can be then added to the orderlines as a one single product consisting of all the items.

Basic Features

  • Odoo's Product pack is an easy to use interface for managing the products in packs.
  • It is fully integrated and helps to create the packs of products.
